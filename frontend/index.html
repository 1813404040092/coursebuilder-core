How It Works

Set your goals:

Tailor courses to your most pressing needs. Create a learning plan that’s right for you whether you want to gain new skills on multimedia or investigative journalism or learn how to access new digital revenue streams.

Learn from experience:

Watch video tutorials from the world’s best digital journalism experts. Much of the instructional material was developed within Africa. The hands-on data journalism training materials which complement Google’s online tutorials were developed and tested in the Sudan and Kenya by the World Bank.

Apply your knowledge:

Test what you have learned and get practical easy to use tips and resources for your next digital journalism projects.

Track your progress:

Your personal dashboard will help keep you motivated and on track with your learning plan.

Keep Going:

Need help fast while on a deadline? Tailor courses to your most pressing needs and access video explainers, concise tip-sheets and exercises to immediately use the needed tool in minutes.
